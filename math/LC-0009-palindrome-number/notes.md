# LC-0009 Palindrome Number

## Key Insight
Palindrome means reading same forward and backward.

## Why my approach worked / failed
- String approach works but uses extra space
- Doesn’t follow constraint of no conversion

## Pattern
Two pointers / digit manipulation

## When to use this again
- When checking symmetry
- When reversing numbers or digits

## Alternative Thinking
- Reverse only half of the number instead of full reversal
- Avoid overflow and extra space
